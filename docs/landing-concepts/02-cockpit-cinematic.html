— Why this matters

Every recommendation surface renders the same five-element sequence: clear risk → prescriptive move → quoted evidence → temporal proof → accountable decision. No score in isolation. No paraphrased evidence. No filled SaaS button competing with the reasoning that earned it.

The architecture treats reading, interpreting, and acting as one cognitive loop — not three product surfaces. Memory on the left so the rep doesn't lose context across weeks. Operating decisions in the center so the next move is unambiguous. Execution on the right because the rep is not delegating thinking — they are delegating synthesis labor.
