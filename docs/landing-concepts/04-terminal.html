// how it works   three primitives, no AI magic, no chatbot
01. READ

Every artifact on the deal becomes substrate.

Calls, emails, Slack threads, calendar invites, CRM activity. Mallin reconstructs operational state from what is already there — without asking the rep to log anything new.

02. INTERPRET

Behavior is the signal. Activity is the noise.

Signer disengagement, champion airtime collapse, procurement entered late, competitor introduced during pricing. Mallin measures what the deal is actually doing — not what the CRM says it is doing.

03. ACT

The manager is in the thread before the rep can skirt.

High-severity alerts post into the deal Slack thread the manager is already in — gap named in plain language, verbatim next question attached. The agent decides what's a gap. The team decides the response.
