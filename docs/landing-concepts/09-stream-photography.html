— II. The system

The CRM stores what was typed.
Mallin reads what was heard.

Every call, every email, every Slack thread, every calendar invite on every active deal — reconstructed into operational state, surfaced as verification alerts in the deal Slack thread the manager is already in.

No new logging. No new workflow. No chatbot. The substrate the rep produces by doing the deal is the substrate Mallin runs on. The agent decides what's a gap. The team decides the response. Approval gate on every CRM write. Stage, amount, close date, and forecast category are never auto-written.
