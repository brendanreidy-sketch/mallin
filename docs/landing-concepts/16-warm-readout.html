01How it works
— READ

Every artifact on the deal becomes substrate.

Calls, emails, Slack threads, calendar invites, CRM activity. Mallin reconstructs the operational state from what's already there — without asking the rep to log anything new.

— INTERPRET

Behavior is the signal. Activity is the noise.

Signer engagement decay, champion airtime collapse, procurement entered late, competitor introduced during pricing. Mallin measures what the deal is actually doing — not what the CRM says it's doing.

— ACT

The manager is in the thread before the rep can skirt.

High-severity alerts post into the deal Slack thread the manager is already in — gap named in plain language, verbatim next question attached. The agent decides what's a gap. The team decides the response.
