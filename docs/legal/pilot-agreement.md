# Mallin — Design Partner Pilot Agreement

**STATUS: DRAFT — LAWYER REVIEW REQUIRED BEFORE FIRST USE**

Starting-point template for design-partner pilots. Lawyer review needed
on: term length, IP scope, liability cap, data-handling commitments,
governing law, conversion mechanics.

---

This Design Partner Pilot Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into
as of **[EFFECTIVE DATE]** (the "Effective Date") by and between:

- **[MALLIN LEGAL ENTITY]**, a **[ENTITY TYPE]** with its principal
  office at **[ADDRESS]** ("Mallin"), and
- **[CUSTOMER LEGAL NAME]**, a **[ENTITY TYPE]** with its principal
  office at **[CUSTOMER ADDRESS]** ("Customer").

## 1. The Pilot

Mallin will provide Customer with access to the Mallin platform (the
"Service") on a no-cost basis for the Pilot Period in exchange for
Customer's participation in product development, feedback, and reasonable
visibility into how the Service is used by Customer's team.

The Service includes: AI-driven pre-call briefs, stakeholder
intelligence surfacing, call analysis (when transcripts are provided),
note-taking with CRM write-back, and a conversational agent layer
("Ask Mallin"). The current capabilities and limitations of the Service
are described at https://mallin.io and in the documentation provided to
Customer.

## 2. Pilot Period

The Pilot Period begins on the Effective Date and continues for
**[ONE HUNDRED EIGHTY (180) DAYS]** unless extended in writing by both
Parties or terminated earlier per Section 9.

## 3. Customer's Participation

Customer agrees to:

(a) designate at least one but no more than **[TEN (10)]** users to
    use the Service during the Pilot Period;
(b) provide Mallin with reasonable feedback on the Service's
    usefulness, defects, and feature requests, at a cadence of at
    least **[ONE (1) STRUCTURED FEEDBACK SESSION PER MONTH]**;
(c) permit Mallin to observe usage data (anonymized session telemetry,
    feature adoption) consistent with the Privacy Policy at
    https://mallin.io/privacy;
(d) participate in case-study or testimonial content only with
    Customer's prior written approval.

## 4. Fees

The Service is provided to Customer at no cost during the Pilot Period.
No fees are owed by Customer to Mallin during the Pilot Period.

If Customer wishes to continue using the Service after the Pilot
Period, the Parties will discuss commercial terms in good faith.
Customer has no obligation to enter into a paid agreement.

## 5. Data Handling

Mallin will handle Customer's data in accordance with: (a) the Privacy
Policy at https://mallin.io/privacy, (b) the AI Governance Policy at
https://mallin.io/ai-governance, and (c) the Security commitments at
https://mallin.io/security. These documents are incorporated by
reference and form part of this Agreement.

Specifically:

(a) **Ownership.** Customer retains all rights to its data
    ("Customer Data") uploaded to or generated in the Service.
(b) **License to Mallin.** Customer grants Mallin a limited,
    non-exclusive, non-transferable license to process Customer Data
    solely to deliver the Service to Customer and improve future
    briefs **within Customer's tenant only**.
(c) **No training.** Mallin will NOT use Customer Data to train
    shared AI models. Mallin will NOT share Customer Data with any
    other Mallin customer.
(d) **Subprocessors.** Mallin uses the subprocessors listed at
    https://mallin.io/subprocessors. Customer is notified by email
    at least **[FOURTEEN (14) DAYS]** in advance of any addition.
(e) **Deletion.** On Customer's written request or expiration of
    this Agreement, Mallin will delete Customer Data from active
    systems within **[THIRTY (30) DAYS]** and from backups within an
    additional **[THIRTY (30) DAYS]**.

## 6. Confidentiality

Each Party agrees that during and after the Pilot Period it will not
disclose the other Party's Confidential Information (as defined in the
Mutual NDA between the Parties, or if none exists, as defined by the
standard meaning of that term in the technology industry) except: (a)
to employees, contractors, and advisors with a need to know, or (b) as
required by law (with prompt notice to the disclosing party where
permitted).

## 7. Intellectual Property

(a) **Mallin's IP.** Mallin retains all rights to the Service, including
    the software, AI models, prompts, UI, documentation, and any
    improvements derived from feedback or usage patterns.
(b) **Customer's IP.** Customer retains all rights to Customer Data.
(c) **Feedback.** Customer grants Mallin a perpetual, royalty-free
    license to use feedback (general comments, feature suggestions,
    bug reports) to improve the Service. This does NOT include
    Customer Data.
(d) **No trademark use.** Neither Party may use the other's name,
    logo, or trademarks without prior written consent (except as
    necessary to perform this Agreement).

## 8. Disclaimers; Limitation of Liability

(a) THE SERVICE IS PROVIDED "AS IS" AND "AS AVAILABLE." MALLIN
    DISCLAIMS ALL WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING
    MERCHANTABILITY, FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE, AND
    NON-INFRINGEMENT.
(b) AI OUTPUTS CAN BE INCORRECT. Customer is responsible for
    reviewing AI outputs before relying on them for material
    business decisions.
(c) TO THE FULLEST EXTENT PERMITTED BY LAW, MALLIN'S AGGREGATE
    LIABILITY ARISING FROM OR RELATING TO THIS AGREEMENT IS LIMITED
    TO **[ONE THOUSAND DOLLARS ($1,000) USD]**. NEITHER PARTY IS
    LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR
    PUNITIVE DAMAGES.

## 9. Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement for any reason on
**[THIRTY (30) DAYS]** written notice. Either Party may terminate
immediately for material breach if not cured within **[FIFTEEN (15)
DAYS]** of written notice.

On termination, Customer's access to the Service ends. Sections 5
(Data Handling), 6 (Confidentiality), 7 (Intellectual Property), 8
(Disclaimers; Limitation of Liability), 10 (General) survive.

## 10. General

(a) **Governing Law.** This Agreement is governed by the laws of
    **[STATE]**, excluding its conflict-of-law principles. Disputes
    will be resolved in the state and federal courts located in
    **[CITY, STATE]**.
(b) **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement (together with the
    incorporated policies) is the entire agreement between the
    Parties regarding the Service during the Pilot Period.
(c) **Amendment.** Only in writing signed by both Parties.
(d) **Assignment.** Neither Party may assign this Agreement without
    the other's written consent (not to be unreasonably withheld),
    except in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of
    substantially all assets.
(e) **Notices.** All notices must be in writing and delivered to
    the addresses above (and for Mallin, with a copy to
    hello@mallin.io).
(f) **No agency.** The Parties are independent contractors. Nothing
    in this Agreement creates a partnership, joint venture, or
    employment relationship.
(g) **Counterparts; Electronic Signatures.** This Agreement may be
    executed in counterparts, including by electronic signature.
